# Manual Domain Registration Guide

## Domain: ebankwallet.com

### Current Status
- **Domain**: ebankwallet.com
- **Status**: Available for registration
- **Registrar**: Porkbun
- **API**: No registration endpoint available

### Manual Registration Steps

#### Step 1: Access Porkbun Website
1. Go to [https://porkbun.com](https://porkbun.com)
2. Click "Sign In" or "Create Account" if you don't have one

#### Step 2: Search for Domain
1. In the search box, type: `ebankwallet.com`
2. Click "Search" or press Enter
3. Verify the domain is available

#### Step 3: Add to Cart
1. Click "Add to Cart" for ebankwallet.com
2. Select registration period (1 year recommended)
3. Choose any additional services if needed

#### Step 4: Complete Registration
1. Click "Checkout" or "Proceed to Checkout"
2. Fill in your contact information:
   - Registrant Name
   - Organization (if applicable)
   - Address
   - Phone Number
   - Email Address

#### Step 5: Payment
1. Enter your credit card information
2. Review the order total
3. Click "Complete Purchase"

#### Step 6: Confirmation
1. You'll receive a confirmation email
2. The domain will be registered to your account
3. DNS settings will be available in your Porkbun dashboard

### Post-Registration Setup

#### DNS Configuration
1. Log into your Porkbun account
2. Go to "Domain Management"
3. Click on ebankwallet.com
4. Configure DNS records as needed

#### API Access (Optional)
1. In domain details, enable "API Access"
2. Use your existing API keys for domain management
3. Available API endpoints:
   - DNS record management
   - SSL certificate management
   - Domain information retrieval

### Important Notes

- **Credit Card**: Ensure your credit card is enabled and has sufficient funds
- **Contact Information**: Use accurate information for domain registration
- **DNS**: Configure DNS records after registration for website functionality
- **Renewal**: Set up auto-renewal to prevent domain expiration

### Troubleshooting

#### Domain Not Available
- The domain may have been registered by someone else
- Check for typos in the domain name
- Consider alternative domain names

#### Payment Issues
- Verify credit card information
- Check with your bank for any restrictions
- Try a different payment method

#### Registration Errors
- Contact Porkbun support if registration fails
- Check email for any error messages
- Verify account information

### Next Steps After Registration

2. **Enable API Access**: For programmatic domain management
3. **Set Up Monitoring**: Use the domain catcher system for monitoring
4. **Website Setup**: Point the domain to your web hosting
